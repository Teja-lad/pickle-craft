Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery timings?

+

We deliver Monday to Saturday between 9 AM to 7 PM. Orders placed before 2 PM are usually delivered the next business day.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Currently, we only ship within India. However, we're working on expanding our services internationally. Stay tuned!

How long do pickles last?

+

Our pickles have a shelf life of 6-12 months when stored properly in a cool, dry place. Always use a clean, dry spoon to avoid contamination.

Can I customize my order?

+

Yes! We offer customization for bulk orders. Contact us directly to discuss your requirements and we'll be happy to help.

What is your return policy?

+

If you receive a damaged product, please contact us within 24 hours with photos. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund.
